A custom solution built from scratch.

When a family needed a better way to run their household, track their kids' daily missions, manage a homeschool schedule, plan date nights, and coordinate a Trail Life troop — there was no app for that. So we built one.

Systems Seven is a six-app family operating system — built entirely from scratch, hosted for free, running on a real-time database, and installed on every family member's phone like a native app. It does things no off-the-shelf tool does, because it was built for one specific family's specific problems.

How We Work

Simple process.
Real results.

No tech jargon. No mystery. You describe your problem, we build the solution, you own it completely.

01
You tell us your problem

Text or email us what's not working. It doesn't have to be technical — just describe the situation. We ask a few follow-up questions to understand what you actually need versus what you think you need.

02
We scope it and give you a number

No guessing. Before any work starts you get a clear description of what we'll build, what it will cost, and how long it will take. You decide if you want to move forward — no pressure either way.

03
We build it, you review it

We build your solution and walk you through it. You tell us what to adjust. We don't consider it done until it actually solves the problem we set out to solve.

04
You own it completely

No monthly platform fees, no proprietary lock-in, no holding your site hostage. What we build is yours. Most sites host for free. You keep every dollar your business makes.

05
We stay available

Need something changed six months later? Text us. We offer affordable monthly maintenance or simple per-change pricing — whatever works for your situation.
